1
Header Panel
Aurora image, moon phase,
chase quality summary.
Updates every 30–60 min.
2
Bz Reading
Negative = southward =
aurora-driving. More
negative = stronger event.
3
Chase Quality
Intensity · moon interference
· astro darkness combined
into one label.
4
Timeline Bar
Bz trace, solar wind V & n,
sun/moon shading. Tap any
hour to jump to it.
5
Search
Find any city, park,
or address. Map flies
to selected result.
6
Night Vision
Red filter over entire app
to preserve dark-adapted
vision in the field.
7
Camera Advisor
Tap → tap map location →
get ISO, shutter, aperture
for your exact device.
8
Clear Sky Finder
8-hour avg cloud heatmap.
Teal = clearest windows.
Pair with Clouds layer.
9
G Storm Badge
Geomagnetic storm level
G1–G5. Updates per
selected forecast hour.
10
HSS Badge
High Speed Stream alert.
Active when solar wind
exceeds 450 km/s.
11
Location Pins
Green = clear & dark.
Red = cloudy or
light-polluted. Tap for details.
12
Layer Controls
Toggle Clouds, Bortle,
Ovation, Locations,
Live Cams, Active Hunt.
13
Report Aurora
Submit a live sighting.
GPS auto-fills. Ring appears
on map for all users.
14
Place Pin
Submit a new dark sky
spot for community review.
Cursor goes crosshair.
15
Admin
Enter passphrase → GO.
Orange badge = items
waiting in queue.
16
Time Slider
Scrub through 8-hour
cloud & space weather
forecast. Tap NOW to reset.
🌌
Seeing aurora?
Tap REPORT AURORA → confirm sighting → ring appears for everyone instantly
☁️
Planning tonight?
Activate Clear Sky Finder → teal zones = best 8-hour windows in the region
📷
Camera settings?
Tap camera icon → tap your location → get ISO, shutter & aperture instantly
📍
Found a great spot?
Tap PLACE PIN → crosshair cursor → tap map → submit for community
📹
Live cams
Toggle Live Cams layer → tap any marker → refreshing snapshot + Watch Live
🌙
In the field?
Tap moon icon → red filter protects your dark-adapted vision at the site
